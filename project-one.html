After Life Chaos

A couch-play style game where you can compete with your friend. One player becomes an angel, while the other becomes a devil. Their goal is to attract the souls of the dead to their own side. To do this, they compete in different challenges, and the winner takes all the souls.

Engine: Unreal EngineGenre: Co-opPlatform: PCTech Stack: Blueprint

The game can be played by two players using either controllers or a keyboard. It’s a fun game where you can compete with your friend through various mini-games. First, you play a ping-pong match where obstacles on the map constantly change. Then, you navigate mazes on your side to collect the souls in the center. Each time you collect a soul, the maze on your side changes, making it impossible to memorize. Finally, you race and collect the souls placed along the track, and the player who finishes 3 laps first earns extra points. At the end of all these games, the player with the highest score wins.
