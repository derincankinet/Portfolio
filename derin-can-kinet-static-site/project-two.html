Alchemist (OnGoing)

A single-player simulation game with RPG elements set in a fantasy medieval world. Satisfy your customers, grow your shop, and become the best merchant in the kingdom.

Engine: Unreal EngineGenre: SimulationPlatform: PCTech Stack: Blueprint

In this single-player game, your goal is to make a profit from your shop and expand it. Your customers will come from different backgrounds and have different needs. If you give them the wrong type of potion, they may get angry, damage your reputation, or even die in some cases… However, if you provide them with a more suitable and helpful potion, they will be grateful and increase your reputation. As your reputation grows, the number of customers increases, making it harder to manage all the chaos. You can hire assistants from the surrounding areas to help you and improve their skills over time.
