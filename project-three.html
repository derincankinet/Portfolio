TREN

Dynasty Warriors

Cut down the enemies coming at you in various ways, and surprise them by quickly changing your attack direction with swift moves.

Engine: Unreal EngineGenre: CombatPlatform: VRTech Stack: VR Blueprint

In this single-player VR game, your goal is to defeat waves of enemies coming at you and reach the next level. You can use various bombs found on the map, parry enemies with your sword, or change your attack direction at the last moment to strike. You can also carry the bodies of defeated enemies before they disappear and use them to attack others, creating different combat styles.
