Warlords

A boardgame where you can challenge your friends as a king, build army, roads, villages and FIGHT!

BoardgameMultiplayerStrategy

In the 3–4 player board game Warlords, your goal is to rule your own kingdom and expand across the map, capturing nearby villages to become the greatest kingdom. To achieve this, you must build armies, fight other players or form alliances, engage in trade, and strengthen your economy. The game is played in turns. When it’s your turn, you should first carry out any construction you plan to do, then move your army, and finally initiate diplomatic interactions with other players.
